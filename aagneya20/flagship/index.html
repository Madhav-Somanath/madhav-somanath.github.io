IEEE InApp Best Engineer

Skills beyond books are an engineer's greatest treasure. The much awaited arena is here for all the engineer geeks out there. IEEE GECBH SB in association with InApp Technologies under AAGNEYA '20 presents, IEEE-InApp Best Engineer - an opportunity to showcase and test your aptitude for engineering. Win exciting prizes and moreover, the winners will have an opportunity to GET PLACED at InApp Technologies! So hone your skills and step right in, a learning experience and a plethora of benefits awaits you! Learn More





EVENT GUIDLINES

  1. Ensure you have your college ID card with you during the contest.

  2. The participants will advance to each round based on their performance in the previous round.

  3. The event consists of four rounds.

    • Round I: This preliminary round will have 50 random questions testing the participant in general aptitude, basic engineering concepts and also the proficiency in language. The test will be of 90 minutes duration. Only a selected number of participants will advance to the next round.
    • Round II: The participants will be divided into a group of three and will be given a topic(technical/general). They will be given 20 - 30 minutes time for preparation and they should present it before the panel of judges. This round is to test the ability of the participant to function as a team.
    • Round III: Here a task(according to their engineering field) is asked to be solved which will be like a hands on session in their respective department workshops.
    • Round IV : An interview round. HR officials from InApp will be conducting this round.
